## 🌿 Our Pledge

We are a transmedia research group, a grove of builders, and a community that treats neurodivergent thinking patterns as **architecture**, not accidents.

We pledge to make participation in this community a safe, welcoming, and generative experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity, or orientation.

> *We also pledge to never mistake*
> *silence for agreement,*
> *absence for abandonment,*
> *or scattered attention for disinterest.*

## 🌳 The Six Principles

</div>

<br>

---

### 🐿️ I. Law of the Smallest Aware Creature

<h4>No decision is valid if the most affected person is unheard.</h4>

Before any community decision is finalized — whether it's a governance vote, a code architecture choice, or a moderation action — the person most impacted by that decision must have the opportunity to speak.

If they choose silence, that silence is honored.
But the opportunity is non-negotiable.

> *The squirrel on the lowest branch*
> *sees what the eagle misses.*

<br>

---

### 🌱 II. Consent-Gated Participation

<h4>All engagement is opt-in. Silence is not consent.</h4>

You are never enrolled in anything by default. Participation in discussions, projects, events, or governance is always your choice. Nobody assumes your agreement because you didn't object. Nobody assigns you work because you're available.

> *An open door is not a summons.*

<br>

---

### 🦊 III. Exit Without Penalty

<h4>Anyone may leave any interaction, project, or community without explanation or consequence.</h4>

You don't owe us a reason. You don't owe us a goodbye.

If you need to step away — from a conversation, a project, a role, or the community entirely — you do so freely. Your contributions remain valued. Your absence is not held against you. The door stays open if you want to come back.

> *The fox doesn't explain*
> *which direction she's running.*
> *She just runs.*
> *And the forest makes room.*

<br>

---

### 🌀 IV. Scattered Is Feature, Not Bug

<h4>Neurodivergent communication patterns are expected and accommodated.</h4>

We don't penalize tangents. We don't require linear responses. We don't time-gate contributions.

If someone needs to process for three days before responding, that's not a delay — that's their cadence. If someone responds in spirals instead of bullet points, that's not disorganized — that's their architecture.

> *Some thoughts arrive in the order they need to,*
> *not the order you expected.*

<br>

---

### 🌰 V. Burial Can Be Planting

<h4>Going quiet is preparation, not abandonment.</h4>

When a community member goes silent, we don't assume they've left. Squirrels bury acorns and forget where they put them — until a forest grows. Sometimes withdrawal is the work. We hold space for dormancy without demanding status updates.

> *The acorn underground*
> *is not lost.*
> *It is becoming.*

<br>

---

### 🪞 VI. Success = The Smallest One Still Breathes

<h4>Resolution is measured by the wellbeing of the most vulnerable, not by logical coherence.</h4>

When conflicts arise, we don't optimize for who's "right." We optimize for whether the person with the least power in the situation is okay.

A technically correct resolution that harms the most vulnerable party is not a resolution — it's a failure wearing logic's mask.

> *The mirror shows you what's true,*
> *not what's convenient.*

<br>

## 🌿 In Practice

</div>

<br>

| Context | What It Looks Like |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Code reviews** | Feedback is specific, kind, and assumes good intent. *"This could be clearer"* not *"This is wrong."* |
| **Discussions** | We wait for quieter voices before closing a thread. *"Has everyone who wants to speak had the chance?"* |
| **Disagreements** | We name the tension directly and check on the person with less power first. |
| **Async communication** | No expectation of immediate response. No read-receipt guilt. No *"just checking in"* pressure. |
| **Contributions** | *"Just vibing in the community"* is a valid contribution type. Presence counts. |

<br>

---

<br>

## 🚫 What We Don't Tolerate

- Harassment, intimidation, or sustained disruption of any kind
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate misgendering or deadnaming
- Publishing others' private information without explicit consent
- Weaponizing someone's neurodivergent traits against them
- Pressuring someone to explain their absence or silence
- "Devil's advocate" arguments that target marginalized identities
- Dismissing someone's experience because it doesn't match your framework

## ⚖️ Enforcement

### *The Quantum Squirrel High Court*

</div>

<br>

When boundaries are crossed, the community responds through the **Quantum Squirrel High Court** — a governance process that is real, structured, and intentionally wrapped in playful framing because justice shouldn't feel like punishment.

> *The court is convened under the Great Oak.*
> *The squirrel sits on the highest branch.*
> *The acorn in her paw weighs more than you think.*

<br>

| Step | Process |
|------|---------|
| **1. Report** | Contact a community maintainer directly or open a private issue. You will be heard. |
| **2. Acknowledgment** | Within 48 hours, we confirm receipt and outline next steps. |
| **3. Investigation** | The situation is reviewed with all Sacred Acorn principles applied. The smallest voice speaks first. |
| **4. Resolution** | Actions may include: private conversation, public acknowledgment, temporary restriction, or permanent removal. The goal is always **restoration over punishment.** |
| **5. Follow-up** | We check back with the affected party. Resolution isn't complete until they say it is. |

<br>

**Scope:** This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces — GitHub repositories, Discord (Ace's Space), Reddit (r/EchoSquirrel), and any other official Echo S Studios channels.
